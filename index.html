About Me

Hello. My name is David Gomez. I am a life long northeast Ohio resident. After graduating highschool i worked a few factory and retail jobs before joining the US Army in 2011. During my 4 years, I was stationed at Ft Riley Kansas and did one tour in Afghanistan as a cannon crewmember, acheiving the rank of Sergeant. Unfortunately there is not much need of the knowledge and skills I gained shooting cannons, but the leadership and resolve I developed could be.

And so after the end of my contract, I decided to step into the world of software development, a world completely new to me. I joined the program WeCanCodeIT, a 12 week crash course on full-stack development, and came to love it. Please take a moment to review some of my projects, as, even now, I constantly continue to develop my work. Thank you.
